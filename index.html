What the software does

A coaching system that improves behaviour and fixes the knowledge behind the work.

CompleteLoop gives managers a structured coaching system, not just a place to store notes. It guides coaching toward specific observable behaviours, confirms whether those behaviours changed, and turns repeated coaching evidence into a clear signal for what the business needs to fix.

What managers do

  • Document the conversation in minutes.

FAQ

Coaching documentation software, in plain English.

What is CompleteLoop?

CompleteLoop is coaching documentation software that helps managers coach in a structured way, follow up, verify behaviour change, and reveal the knowledge, training, process, and accountability gaps behind repeat performance problems.

Is CompleteLoop just a coaching app?

No. The coaching workflow is the front end. The bigger value is the signal it creates. When the same coaching themes keep appearing, CompleteLoop helps the business see what knowledge, training, standard, SOP, or process needs to be corrected.

How does CompleteLoop help managers coach better?

It guides managers to coach specific observable behaviours, document the conversation, set follow-up, verify whether the behaviour changed, and recognize improvement when it sticks.

How does CompleteLoop improve the knowledge loop?

Coaching shows where the business knowledge does not match reality. CompleteLoop captures those patterns so training, SOPs, standards, and support systems can be updated before the same issue keeps repeating.

Who is CompleteLoop for?

CompleteLoop is designed for service businesses, CX teams, BPOs, contact centers, franchises, and multi-location teams where manager coaching, consistent execution, and customer experience matter.
