The problem

If coaching is invisible, the business cannot learn from it.

Some managers coach consistently. Some do not. Some follow up. Some forget. Leaders see scores, complaints, defects, turnover, and missed KPIs, but they rarely see the coaching activity and behaviour-change evidence behind those outcomes.

Coaching may or may not be happening consistently, but leadership usually has no clean way to know.
Managers may start coaching conversations, but follow-up is often inconsistent or invisible.
Behaviour change is too often assumed from the next scorecard instead of verified through a closed loop.
Repeated coaching themes stay trapped in conversations instead of becoming training, knowledge, process, and accountability signals.

The MVP

A simple coaching workflow that ensures the loop actually closes.

CompleteLoop is built for the manager first. It prompts coaching, documents the conversation, schedules follow-up, verifies behaviour change, and turns the outcome into useful business evidence.

1. PromptMake the expected coaching action clear.
2. DocumentCapture the behaviour, coaching, and agreed action.
3. Follow upSet a date to check whether behaviour changed.
4. VerifyConfirm the outcome instead of assuming.
5. SignalUse repeated patterns to show what needs improvement.

The signal layer

Every closed coaching loop creates operational evidence.

CompleteLoop does not start by replacing your KMS, LMS, HRIS, or dashboards. It gives those systems a better input: verified coaching data from the people closest to the work.
